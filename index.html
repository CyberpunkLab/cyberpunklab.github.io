Cyberpunk Lab

The complexity of controlling machineries with sophisticated rules in manufacturing and hard technologies makes them a good candidate for Reinforcement Learning (RL). Using RL has the potential to generate billions of dollars in increased performance and efficiency. RL in manufactruring will not only optimize the rules of operating for more efficiency and time saving, it also can disrupt manufacturing by developing new methods that require less complicated machinery. Cyberpunk Lab is developing a RL service to automate development of industrial controls.
