Machina Labs

The complexity of controlling pieces of machinery with sophisticated rules in manufacturing and hard technologies makes them a good candidate for Machine Learning (ML). Using ML has the potential to generate billions of dollars in increased performance and efficiency. ML in manufacturing will not only optimize the rules of operating for more efficiency and time saving, but it also can disrupt manufacturing by developing new methods that require less complicated machinery. Machina Labs is developing an ML service to automate development of industrial controls.
